About Us

At Omkar Agro Fresh Farm, we are passionate about bringing the pure essence of Indian agriculture to your kitchen. Founded with a commitment to quality and authenticity, our company specializes in the manufacturing of vegetable powders and traditional Indian masalas through advanced dehydration and blending techniques. We combine modern food processing technology with age-old Indian culinary wisdom to deliver products that are rich in flavor, nutrients, and aroma — without any added preservatives or artificial ingredients. From beetroot and ginger powder to garam masala and pav bhaji masala, our catalog is a tribute to the diversity and richness of Indian food culture. Each batch is made with farm-fresh ingredients, carefully processed to ensure long shelf life while retaining the natural taste and color.
